A DIARY FULL OF POEMS



Women- The Epitome of Resilience

Lysandra D'Souza, Dec 1, 2020

It’s Known The Best By Quite A Few;
That Whatever Given To A Woman,
She Gives It Back Multiplied Into Two!

I Give To You Just A Cliched Hint,
To Let You Know My Opinion’s Tint.

As a Child If You Give Her Education,
Be Rest Assured About Literacy In Your Entire Generation.

She Readily Makes Any Niché Feel Like Home,
Where Dullness Will No Longer Freely Roam.

Her Love For You Will Be Pure And Passionate,
Provided You Give Her, Her Respect Definite.

She Fills The Void Of a Father, Sister or Brother,
But Her Place Can Be Taken By None Other.

Even Though Many Treat Her Brutally Out Of Misogny,
She Holds The Tolerance To Control Her Agony.

She Still Treats You As Nature’s Equal Part,
But Cross The Limit And She’ll Tear You Apart.

Despite the Gender Biased Role On Her You Implicate,
Trust Me You’re In Real Trouble The Moment She Plans To Retaliate.

She’s Loving Yet a Fierce Soul,
Red And Flaring Like The Burning Charcoal;

Definitely One Day The Whole World She’ll Lead,
Because She Can Do More Than Twice What Men Do, While She Bleeds.
